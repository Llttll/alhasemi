about us

AL-Hashemi Group Company For General Trading, Contracting and Oil Services Ltd.

Is one of the leading companies in the field of construction, mechanical and commercial affairs and It was established in the province of Baghdad since 2007. It is necessary to follow the world in terms of development and like all the successful companies and institutions in the world wave a goal to aspire to and since there is a vision and philosophy to guide and achieve the goal, we are keen to apply our philosophy and vision in the business world, this understanding has helped to develop our human, technical and administrative resources in addition to keeping pace With the world in using and possessing the latest technology and mechanisms to develop the great Iraq. It is important for us to strive to honor Iraqi identity by signing contracts with Iraqi companies and leading international companies in the world. As an EPC provider, we offer end-to-end project execution, integrating engineering expertise, efficient procurement, and high-quality construction to deliver complex energy projects on time and within budget. With a commitment to operational efficiency and sustainable growth, we combine advanced technology, industry know-how, and a highly skilled workforce to meet the evolving needs of our clients
