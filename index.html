about us

We are al-hashemi group company general trading & contracting ltd.

Al-Hashemi Group Company, established in Baghdad in 2007, is a leading firm in construction, mechanical, and commercial sectors.The company prioritizes development, leveraging advanced technology and resources to contribute to Iraq's growth. Guided by a clear vision and philosophy, Al-Hashemi Group strives to honor Iraqi identity by collaborating with local and international companies to achieve excellence in its operations.
